📖 About LoanCalc

Our Mission

LoanCalc was born with a simple but powerful mission: democratize access to professional financial tools so that anyone can make informed decisions about their loans and investments.

We believe everyone deserves to fully understand the financial implications before committing to a loan, mortgage, or any financial product.

What We Do?

We offer free and accurate financial calculators that help you:

  • Calculate personal loans: Know your exact monthly payments and total interest you'll pay
  • Plan mortgages: Visualize your complete amortization table for long-term decisions
  • Compare options: Try different scenarios before deciding
  • Understand your finances: Learn how interest and amortization work

Why Trust Us?

🔒 Total Privacy

Your data NEVER leaves your browser. All calculations are performed locally on your device. We don't store any personal financial data.

💯 100% Free

All our tools are completely free. No registrations, no hidden charges, no tricks. Just useful tools available to everyone.

⚡ Accurate Calculations

We use the same formulas that banks and professional financial institutions use. You can trust the accuracy of our results.

📱 Accessible From Anywhere

Responsive design that works perfectly on computers, tablets, and mobile devices. Calculate wherever you want, whenever you want.

Our Story

LoanCalc was created by a team of developers and financial experts who were frustrated with the lack of transparent and easy-to-use tools in the market.

We saw how many people signed loans without fully understanding what they were accepting. We decided to change that by creating tools that empower people with knowledge.

Our Values

  • Transparency: No fine print, no tricks. Everything is clear and straightforward.
  • Accessibility: Financial tools for everyone, without barriers.
  • Education: We don't just calculate, we also explain.
  • Privacy: Your financial information is yours alone.
  • Accuracy: Exact calculations you can trust.
