🎯 Investment Tips

  • Start investing early to maximize compound interest
  • Diversify your investment portfolio
  • Consider dollar-cost averaging
  • Review and rebalance regularly
  • Keep emergency funds separate

📊 Understanding ROI

  • ROI = Return on Investment
  • Measures investment profitability
  • Higher ROI = Better return
  • Compare different investments
  • Consider risk vs. reward

⚠️ Important Considerations

  • Account for taxes on investment gains
  • Consider inflation impact
  • Investment fees reduce returns
  • Risk tolerance varies by individual
