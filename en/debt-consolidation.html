💡 Useful Information

🎯 What is Debt Consolidation?

  • Combines multiple debts into one
  • Single monthly payment
  • Usually lower interest rate
  • Simplifies financial management
  • Can reduce monthly payment

✅ Benefits of Consolidating

  • Save on interest
  • Lower monthly payment
  • Single creditor
  • Better organization
  • Less financial stress

⚠️ Important Considerations

  • Check origination fees
  • Compare rates from multiple lenders
  • Evaluate total term length
  • Don't accumulate new debt
  • Consider your payment ability
