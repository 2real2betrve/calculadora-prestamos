💡 Useful Information

🎯 Auto Loan Tips

  • Put down as much as possible (20-30%)
  • Compare rates from multiple lenders
  • Consider resale value
  • Don't exceed 4-5 years term
  • Include insurance in your budget

📊 What is Residual Value?

  • Estimated value at end of loan
  • Common in leasing or financing
  • Reduces monthly payment
  • Must pay or return at end
  • Typically 20-40% of price

⚠️ Factors to Consider

  • Competitive interest rate
  • Term appropriate for your budget
  • Maintenance costs
  • Vehicle depreciation
  • Additional expenses (insurance, taxes)
