📚 Useful Information

What is a personal loan?

  • Amount of money lent by a financial institution
  • Repaid in fixed monthly installments
  • Includes principal plus interest
  • Typical term: 12 to 84 months

How is the payment calculated?

  • Uses the French amortization formula
  • Constant monthly payments
  • More interest paid at the beginning
  • More principal paid at the end

Important tips

  • Compare offers from different banks
  • Check the APR (Annual Percentage Rate)
  • Read all conditions carefully
  • Don't borrow more than 30-40% of your income
