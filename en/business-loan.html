💡 Useful Information

🎯 Business Loan Tips

  • Maintain positive cashflow at all times
  • Don't commit more than 30% of income
  • Have an emergency business fund
  • Compare rates from different lenders
  • Consider term based on your capacity

📊 What is Cashflow?

  • Money available after expenses
  • Income - Expenses - Loan Payment
  • Must always be positive
  • Indicates business financial health
  • Critical for decision making

⚠️ Important Considerations

  • Stability of monthly income
  • Business seasonality
  • Variable and unexpected expenses
  • Growth capacity
  • Bank collateral requirements
