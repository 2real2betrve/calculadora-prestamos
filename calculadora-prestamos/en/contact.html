Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are the calculators free?

Yes, all our calculators are 100% free and always will be. You don't need to register or pay anything.

Are the calculations accurate?

Yes, we use the same mathematical formulas that banks and financial institutions use. You can trust the accuracy of the results.

Do you store my financial data?

No. All calculations are performed locally in your browser. No financial data you enter is sent to our servers or stored.

Can I use the calculators on my mobile?

Of course! Our site is designed to work perfectly on all devices: mobile, tablets, and computers.

Do you offer loans or financial products?

No. LoanCalc is an independent calculation tool. We don't offer loans nor are we affiliated with any bank or financial institution.

Can I share the results?

You can print the amortization tables using the "Print Table" button on each calculator. You can also take screenshots of the results.

Will you add more calculators?

Yes! We're constantly working on new tools. Coming soon: auto loan calculator, savings, investment, and more.
